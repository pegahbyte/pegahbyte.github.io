Deep Learning with Neural Networks for Image Classification

In this project images of common pigmented skin lesions are classified in either benign or cancerous. Different deep neural network architectures are used both with and without transfer learned weights. For this purpose the "HAM10000" dataset, a large collection of multi-source dermatoscopic images of common pigmented skin lesions is used.
